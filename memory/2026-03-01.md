### 9. Cron Jobs vs Heartbeat Daemon

**Decision:** Keep cron jobs under Kublai, heartbeat daemon handles distributed tasks.

**Why:**
- Cron = high-level, human-facing, strategic
- Heartbeat = operational, distributed, tactical
- Clean separation of concerns
- No redundancy

**Impact:** Kublai coordinates, agents execute.

**Lesson:** Complementary systems > competing systems.

| Decision | Rationale | Trade-off |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
