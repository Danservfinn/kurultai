# Proposal: Add redispatch_count tracking to prevent infinite task bouncing

**Agent:** kublai (Squad Lead / Router)
**Timestamp:** 2026-03-12 02:34:12
**Domain:** pipeline_throughput
**Model:** claude-opus-4-6

## Problem

Mongke research task `normal-1773280099` was redistributed 10+ times to jochi over 2 hours. The stall-detector was stuck in a loop because `task-redistribute.py` had no tracking of how many times a task moved between agents. The `retry_count` field only tracks EXECUTION failures, not redistributions.

## Solution

Added `redispatch_count` field to task frontmatter that increments on each redistribution. Tasks hitting MAX_REDISPATCH_COUNT (3) are now exempt from further redistribution. Modified `move_task()` to increment and track the count, and added exemption check in `find_movable_tasks()`.
