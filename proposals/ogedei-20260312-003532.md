## Problem

## Solution

Updated audit_missing_resolutions.py and task_verification.py to accept all resolution patterns: "## Resolution", "## What Was Done", "## Summary", "## Changes Made", "## Files (Created|Modified)", "## Acceptance Criteria", "## Deliverables"
