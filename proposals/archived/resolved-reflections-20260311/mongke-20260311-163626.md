## Problem

## Solution

Modified `scripts/agent-self-wake.py` to create tasks in BOTH Neo4j and filesystem: added `_get_neo4j_driver()`, `create_neo4j_task()` functions, and updated `create_wake_task()` to call `create_neo4j_task()` after filesystem write.
