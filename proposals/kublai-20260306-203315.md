## Problem

Agent context.md files grow unboundedly with work history and are loaded into every task execution, wasting ~1-2K tokens per task. Mongke's was 8.2KB, chagatai's 4.0KB — never audited or compacted.

## Solution

Added check_context_bloat() and fix_context_bloat() to scripts/memory_audit.py. Detection flags context.md >4KB. Fix trims Latest Work/Recent Work sections to 3 most recent items while preserving header, current task, and notes. Wired into run_audit() and the --fix pipeline.
